About Me

I am a Member of Technical Staff 3 at VMware NSX R&D in Palo Alto, CA. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from College of William and Mary, working with Prof. Gang Zhou. Before that I received my M.Eng. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from National Key Laboratory of Pattern Recognition at Institute of Automation, CAS, and my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University.
During my Ph.D., I conducted research in Ubiquitous and Mobile Systems. [Google Scholar Citations]
Here are some topics I have worked on during my Ph.D. :

            - Context-aware Low-bandwidth Video Streaming on Mobile Devices
            - Context-aware WiFi Power Management and I/O Path Optimization on Mobile Devices
            - Human Activity Recognition in Wearable Mobile Systems
            - Graphical User Authentication on Mobile Devices
