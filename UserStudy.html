User Study

Participate in a paid user study


If you are 18 or older, we would like to invite you to participate in a study that helps test
implementations of computational techniques for privacy-preserving mobile authentication. You
will be given a mobile device (e.g., a smartphone) and asked to perform three tasks:

1. Typing task: you will be given a set of general questions that will be presented on the
screen, and asked to answer them using the mobile device
2. Map navigation task: in this task, you will be asked to locate several locations on a
map
3. Reading task: you will be given a few short text documents to read on the mobile
device

At the end of each task, you will be asked a few questions. 

The data will be used for a research study at NYIT and W&M, and will be collected over six 
days. Each session will take between 60 and 120 minutes to complete. At the end of the six 
sessions, you will receive a monetary gift in the amount of $240 for your participation.

If you are interested, please email xqi@email.wm.edu for further information. Date, time and 
location of data collection TBA.

This user study has been approved by the following two protocols.

       Protection of Human Subjects Committee (PHSC) protocol:  PHSC-2013-06-20-8808-gzhou titled A User Study for Smartphone Authentication

       Air Force Protocol Number: FWR20130205X
       Protocol Title: A User Study for Smartphone Authentication
